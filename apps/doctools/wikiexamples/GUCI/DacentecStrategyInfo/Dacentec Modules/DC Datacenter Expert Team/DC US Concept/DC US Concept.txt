h2. Dacentec Concept in production in US

Dacentec constructed already a privately owned datacenter in the US, North Carolina (next to Google in North Carolina on crossing of 2 powergrids). In that datacenters we applied Generation 1 of our new Green Unbreakable Cloud concept and can prove the impact of our solution\!

Generation 1: Modular Datacenter Approach (PODs), Improved Air cooling system
Generation 2: Plate Rack system, Power Orchestrator System, Cloud Management System

In our US Datacenter facility in Lenoir, NC, we can provide 300PB storage capacity (Petabyte = 1024 Terabyte = 1.048.576 GBytes) and 150.000 CPU cores capacity.
