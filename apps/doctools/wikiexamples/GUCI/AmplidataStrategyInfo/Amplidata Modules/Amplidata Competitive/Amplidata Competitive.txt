h2. Amplidata Competitive
 
Online Storage: Many Application Providers started their business on existing storage clouds. E.g.: S3.  The challenge is to convince them to build their own storage infrastructure at a fraction of the cost.
 
Using open source solutions, lots of service providers build scale out storage infrastructure of their own. Key selling points here are lower management cost, higher reliability.
 
There are quite a few “Expensive” enterprise-grade solutions available that can deliver acceptable levels of availability. Key selling point will be cost. E.g.: EMC Atmos, NetApp GX, …
 
A few startups are operating in the same space. E.g.: Cleversafe, Gluster, ...
